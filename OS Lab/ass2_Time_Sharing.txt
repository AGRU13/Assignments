The CPU of your machine, single CPU is there, executes one instruction at a time.
That is, it can execute one process at a time.
So, the execution of the processes are not simultaneous (parallel) rather serial.
The CPU is occupied by a process for a given period of time then it is occupied by
Another process for the next slot ---> round robin

Assignment is to show that the processes are running in a round robin way on the CPU
And find out the slot time.
